GIMP for Braille Shades Art

----- ----- ----- ----- -----

Having the image:

GIMP:
IMG to: 1-Bit Bold Line Art

----- ----- ----- ----- -----

GIMP reset settings (optional):

- Settings:
 1. Delete the version number to restore everything to the default settings
    ~/.config/GIMP/[VERSION.NUMBER]
 2. Change language to English
    Edit > Preferences > Interface > Language > English [en_US]
 3. Disable "Show on Start..." from the 'Welcome to GIMP...'

-----

 Basic hotkeys

Space : move through the image

Zoom + : Ctrl + Mouse wheel up or +
Zoom - : Ctrl + Mouse wheel down or - 

Zoom to original/100%, Scale 1:1):
1 (key)

Center and fit the entire image to the window:
Ctrl + Shift + J (View > Zoom > Fit Image in Window)

-----

 1. Export As... (Shift + Ctrl + E)
[NAME].png

 2. Remove transparencies
Image > Flatten Image

 2. Crop Tool
Shift + C
Enter once adjusted

 3. Delete selected areas
Shift + O Fuzzy Select Tool, Shift = Include new areas, Ctrl = remove areas
or
Shift + E Erased Tool, with Bursh Hardness 100,0, and custom the Brush Size with something like 50,00
Stop selecting areas: Shift + Ctrl + A (Select > None)
or
H to Healing Tool, Ctrl + Click for select area, then Click

 4. Black and White
Image > Mode > Grayscale

 5. Remove grays close to white
Colors > Levels...
Below the 'histogram' there are 3 arrows/triangles; drag the white one on the right towards the center
Alternative method:
Use the white eyedropper tool, when you hover over it, it will say "Pick white point for all channels", lick on the desired gray area to remove

 6. Bold line art
Filters > Generic > Erode
It can be repeated with Ctrl + F and undone with Ctrl + Z

 7. IMG to 1-Bit
1-Bit color: Colors > Local Threshold...

 8. Option to IMG to 2-Bit
Colors > Posterize...
Posterizer Levels: 4
Use  5. Remove grays close to white  to adjust the colors

 9. Make the file smaller by saving only 2 or 4 colors
Image > Mode > Indexed...
Colormap > Generate optimum palette > 2 for 1-Bit, 5 for 2-Bit, is 5 because the white also counts
Dithering > Color dithering: None, other effects is useful for Braille Dots

 10. Scale Image
Image > Scale Image...
Image Size, 1000 px maximum
Quality, Interpolation: None (so that there are no blurry pixels at the edges)
